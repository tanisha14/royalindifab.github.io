About

My name is Megha Agrawal, I was born and brought up in famous business family of Jaipur, Rajasthan. I run my own successful Etsy shop from year 2016. In my shop I sell authentic Jaipur stuff from local vendors and artisans to help them . I gathered an in depth knowledge of sourcing and production and makers of Jaipur items like handicraft,semi precious stones, block print items, fabrics etc. After my graduation luckily I got admission to study marketing in USA. I stayed in the states for one year and during my stay I realized how much people love Jaipur and shopping here but they have to pay multiple times high prices to order from online portals. After My graduation from UC Berkeley California, I came back to Jaipur and decided to help Jaipur shopping admirers worldwide to shop in-store like local at reasonable price

The experience will take place in the local, street markets and registered branded stores of Jaipur. We do not keep any commission in prices so that tourist can also enjoy shopping at very special and bargained prices that are offered to a local shopper. We wanted to bridge this gap between the experience of Jaipur local shopping for a tourist and a native shopper.
